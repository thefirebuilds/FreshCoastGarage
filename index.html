About Us

Hi, I’m Blake. I grew up on the shores of Lake Michigan and have been around cars pretty much since I could walk. I started racing at six — soapbox derby, then ice racing on Lake Winnebago, and eventually into Spec Miata, Mazda2, and Touring 4 MX-5. I've raced all over: Road America (my home track), Blackhawk Farms, Gingerman, Milwaukee Mile (where I still hold a lap record), Mid-Ohio, COTA, Hallett, Lime Rock, and even up at MOSPORT in Canada.

I spent ten years running a Miata salvage yard with my dad in a blue-collar town known for Case Tractors and Johnson Wax. I’ve prepped and maintained my racecars by hand and I bring that same attention to every car in my rental fleet. Whether you're renting for a trip or just need something solid while your car is in the shop, I make sure each car is ready for the unexpected.

I moved to Austin about a decade ago. These days, I’m raising three kids, sailing when I can, brewing beer in the garage, and chasing my rescue dogs around the yard. I also do woodworking, bike around town, and sneak off to snowboard when the mountains call.

Fresh Coast Garage is my way of sharing clean, reliable cars with people who want something better than a typical rental — a bit more personal, a lot more prepared, and run by someone who actually cares about the experience. Welcome to Austin!
