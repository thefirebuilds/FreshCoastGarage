Information we collect

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us or that is necessary to operate our services, including:

  • Name and contact information, such as email address and phone number
  • Reservation, booking, trip, pickup, drop-off, and vehicle-related details
  • Messages, support requests, and operational communications
  • Account administration details for authorized Fresh Coast Garage personnel or administrators
  • Technical and operational information needed to monitor, secure, and support our systems

How we use information

Fresh Coast Garage, LLC uses personal information for legitimate business and operational purposes, including to:

  • Operate and support vehicle hosting, fleet management, and reservation workflows
  • Communicate about bookings, pickup, drop-off, trip support, account issues, and service updates
  • Send operational alerts to authorized account owners or administrators who have opted in
  • Investigate technical issues, ingestion failures, telemetry issues, data mismatches, and other operational exceptions
  • Maintain security, prevent misuse, and comply with legal or platform requirements

SMS messaging and consent

Fresh Coast Garage, LLC may send SMS messages only to recipients who have provided consent or who are authorized account owners or administrators receiving operational alerts related to Fresh Coast Garage services.

SMS messages may include reservation support, pickup or drop-off updates, trip-related service notifications, operational alerts, ingestion failures, telemetry issues, data mismatches, or other account-related notifications.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Recipients may reply STOP to opt out at any time and HELP for assistance.

For more information about SMS consent, see our SMS Consent and Messaging Terms.

No sale or sharing of mobile opt-in information

Fresh Coast Garage, LLC does not sell, rent, trade, or share mobile opt-in information, phone numbers, or SMS consent records with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Mobile opt-in information and consent are used only to provide the messaging services requested or authorized by the recipient, to operate our services, and to comply with applicable messaging requirements.

When we share information

We may share information only when necessary to operate our services, support reservations, maintain our systems, comply with legal obligations, or work with service providers that help us deliver business operations.

Service providers may process information on our behalf, but they are not authorized to use personal information for their own marketing or promotional purposes.

Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and operational safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to limit access to information to those with a legitimate business need.

Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain operational records, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and support legitimate business needs.

Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information, subject to any legal, security, accounting, or operational obligations that require us to retain certain records.

SMS recipients may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. Assistance is available by replying HELP or contacting us directly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
