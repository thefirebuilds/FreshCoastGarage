Fresh Coast Garage SMS Alerts Consent

This page documents SMS consent, opt-in, opt-out, and operational messaging terms for Fresh Coast Garage, LLC. Denmark is Fresh Coast Garage, LLC's internal operations platform used to monitor fleet activity, ingestion health, telemetry issues, booking exceptions, and other operational events related to vehicle hosting.

What these messages are for

Fresh Coast Garage, LLC sends operational SMS alerts to the account owner or designated administrator when Denmark detects issues or exceptions that may require attention.

Examples include:
  • Email polling failures
  • Trip-state inconsistencies
  • Telemetry outages during active trips
  • Missing or failed booking imports
  • Critical payout or data mismatch alerts
These messages are not for:
  • Marketing or promotions
  • Mass messaging
  • Third-party advertising
  • Messages to the general public
  • Messages to users who have not opted in
Message frequency varies based on platform activity.

Who receives these messages

SMS alerts are intended only for the Fresh Coast Garage, LLC account owner or designated administrator who explicitly requests and enables SMS alerts for Fresh Coast Garage operational notifications.

Phone numbers are not added automatically, and consent is not inferred from account ownership alone.

Consent language

The following language is used when enabling SMS alerts for Fresh Coast Garage, LLC operational notifications.

This checkbox is presented as an unchecked, manual opt-in control. Consent is recorded only when the user manually enables SMS alerts.

Sample messages

Fresh Coast Garage alert: Email polling failed 3 times in 15 minutes. No new booking emails have been ingested.

Fresh Coast Garage alert: Juneau telemetry has been stale for 92 minutes during an active trip.

Fresh Coast Garage alert: Winnie booking import completed with missing trip dates. Review required.

Opt-out

Recipients can opt out of SMS alerts at any time by replying STOP. Assistance is available by replying HELP.

SMS alerts may also be disabled directly within Fresh Coast Garage account notification settings.

Privacy and data sharing

Fresh Coast Garage, LLC uses mobile numbers and SMS consent records only to provide operational SMS alerts requested by the account owner or designated administrator.

Mobile opt-in information and consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
